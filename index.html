Being a first-year student at OsloMet

Our experieces of becoming students

This website is made by first-year students at OsloMet,

who tell about their experiences and difficulties.

TOP

Reflection

This page consists of reflection texts describing the challenges that us students have faced this semester. This semester has brought a lot of different scenarios that we have never faced before and needed to solve. The discussion mainly considers the group members´ transition from upper-secondary school to university. The biggest difficulties that we have faced this year is learning to code, the transition from being a pupil to becoming a student, and to manage the workload of the different subjects.

Accessibility

This page consists of everything we did to make our webiste accessibile. Accsessibility is something very important to take into consideration when making an app or a website so that the page can be experienced at an equal level for whoever visits it. This page will run you thorougly through the choices and the different things we implemented to make our webiste as accessibile as it could be.

Topical

This page consists of texts from our subject DATA1100 - Technology and Society for Programmers, that we have written throughout the semester. The theme of the texts are digital cities and the welfare states. The text goes through innovation, and how 3D food engineering can become a future job that can create new and useful values for us humans.
